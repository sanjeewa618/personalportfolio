About Me

Hello! I'm Sanjeewa Sandaruwan, a passionate Computer Science undergraduate with a strong interest in web development, software engineering, and emerging technologies. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura.

I completed my school education at C.W.W. Kannangara Central College, Mathugama, where I cultivated a strong academic background and leadership qualities that continue to shape my approach to problem-solving and teamwork.

With a solid foundation in programming and critical thinking, I enjoy creating efficient, scalable, and user-friendly applications. My goal is to leverage my technical skills and creativity to develop innovative digital solutions that make a real-world impact.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, participating in hackathons, engaging in open-source projects, and taking on leadership roles in student organizations to sharpen both my technical and interpersonal skills.
